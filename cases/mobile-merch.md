Mobile dev practice lead and I decided that developers for each platform deserved their own T-shirt design.

I looked for memes and popular phrases among mobile developers so that they feel connection to this merch.

<div class="large-divider"></div>

## "And adapt it for iPad"

Who hasn't seen an iOS developer's face when PM or designer casually mentions that the app must be supported for an iPad too?

I especially liked the hammer in the XCode logo so I wanted to include it in the story like a loaded gun.
