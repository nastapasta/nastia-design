What I'm about

Biography and fun facts

For years, I’ve been designing experiences through space, education, and collaboration. While studying architecture, I organised educational events for students meet peers from across Europe, and built small projects together. With friends, we hosted a 10-day architecture workshop in a small Belarusian village. My favorite architect is Peter Zumthor, and my personal dream — maybe a little selfish — is to design and build a home for my family.

In the beginning of the final year in the master's program, I discovered UX design and liked it. Eventually, I earned Master degree in architecture. As a tutor for second year students, the group and I explored how human-centered design methods could be applied to urban design.

In my work, I try to live by my values — being strong and kind — in ways that are meaningful for people and beneficial to the businesses that serve them.

English is neither my first nor second language, so I may still make mistakes. Thanks for understanding.
