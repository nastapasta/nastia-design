How I work

Talk to you.

I ask silly questions and try to understand what exactly the problem is. I'm trained in product management, so I'll even ask you why you've decided to do all of this.

Talk to users.

Whenever it's possible, we will go to your users to make sure we do what they actually need (or don't know yet they need it) and to test the solutions.

Work with developers.

My experience is mostly within cross-functional teams, from which I learned to validate the solutions with the dev team as early as possible: good design should be implementable.

I focus on designing products that leverage the best from new technologies to help people do their job well. I am capable of combining product design with product management responsibilities. As a graduate architect, I am a useful asset in products dedicated to emerging technologies in civil engineering, architecture, and urban design.
